# Ceremonia Inaugural - Palabras de Bienvenida MAPLA-MANTEMIN 2024

## Metadata
- **Authors:** Mimy Mackenzie (Gerente de Conferencias, Gecamin)
- **Session:** CI (Ceremonia Inaugural) MAPLA 2024
- **Topic:** Opening ceremony for the 20th anniversary of the MAPLA-MANTEMIN international mining maintenance congress, held October 9-11, 2024 at Hotel Sheraton, Santiago, Chile.

## Key Points
- MAPLA-MANTEMIN 2024 celebrates its 20th anniversary as the premier mining maintenance conference in Latin America
- Conference theme: "Proyectando el Mantenimiento, Avanzando para la Mineria 4.0" (Projecting Maintenance, Advancing for Mining 4.0)
- Conference President: Guillermo Yanez, Gerente Corporativo de Mantenimiento, Antofagasta Minerals (AMSA)
- Organized by Gecamin in collaboration with Universidad de La Serena (Chile), Universidad Industrial de Santander (Colombia), and Universidad Catolica de Santa Maria (Peru)
- 102 committee members from 75 institutions and companies (23 executives, 71 professionals, 10 editorial experts)
- 77 original abstracts received, 67 published in proceedings
- 178 authors from 12 countries contributed
- Technical program includes 78 presentations: 8 plenary talks, 66 technical presentations, 4 posters, and 3 technical courses
- 23 international authors and 7 female authors participated
- 100+ professionals from 30+ mining operations attended
- Attendance split: 88% national (331 Chilean) and 12% international (45 attendees)
- Countries represented: Chile, Peru, USA, Brazil, Spain, Mexico, Germany, Ecuador, Argentina, and others
- Major mining companies represented: Antofagasta Minerals, Codelco, SQM, Teck, Anglo American, Lundin Mining, Chinalco, Collahuasi, Candelaria, among others
- Main sponsor: Los Pelambres (Antofagasta Minerals)
- Platinum sponsors: Innomotics, Russell Mineral Equipment (RME)
- Gold sponsors: ABB, C.C. Jensen, MILE, Simpro, Tecnica Thomas Sargent
- Gecamin has engaged 39,000+ executives and professionals across 26 years of conferences covering water, energy, environment, sustainability, automation, maintenance, and human capital

## Relevance to Asset Management
- MAPLA-MANTEMIN is the largest and most established mining maintenance conference in Latin America, making it a key competitive intelligence source for asset management software and services targeting the mining sector
- The conference connects decision-makers from major mining companies (Codelco, AMSA, Teck, Anglo American) who are potential buyers of maintenance and asset management solutions
- The 2024 theme on Mining 4.0 signals industry-wide digital transformation in maintenance practices
- Strong presence of technology sponsors (ABB, Innomotics, RME) indicates active investment in maintenance technology
